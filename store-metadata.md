Gym Buddy is your complete AI-powered fitness companion, built for serious athletes and beginners alike.

• Log exercises with sets, reps, and weight
• Auto-detect Personal Records (PRs)
• 400+ exercises with muscle targeting
• Active workout timer and volume tracking
• Full workout history and stats

**🥗 NUTRITION TRACKER**
• 220+ Indian foods + global food database
• Barcode scanner for packaged foods
• Calorie & macro tracking (protein, carbs, fat)
• AI-powered nutrition goals calculator
• Weekly nutrition reports

**🎯 HABIT TRACKER**
• Build daily habits with 90-day grids
• Streak tracking and discipline score
• GitHub-style contribution heatmap

**⚡ AI COACH (Powered by Claude)**
• Personalized daily workout suggestions
• Muscle gap analysis and rotation planning
• Progressive overload recommendations
• Full AI chat with your personal coach

• 15 achievement badges
• Level-up celebrations
• Discipline score

**📊 STATS & REPORTS**
• Year-round activity heatmap
• Volume trend charts

• PR history leaderboard

**👥 SOCIAL**
• Connect with training partners
• Friends leaderboard (weekly volume)
• Activity feed with reactions
• Privacy-first design

**Works offline** — all your data syncs when connected.
